# Subscription FAQ

Q: Can I upgrade my subscription?

A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately after payment confirmation.

Q: Can I downgrade my subscription?

A: Yes. Downgrades become active at the next billing cycle.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?

A: Yes. Cancellation prevents future billing but does not provide automatic refunds.

Q: Can I reactivate a cancelled subscription?

A: Yes. Users may reactivate through the account settings page.

Q: Where can invoices be downloaded?

A: Invoices are available in the billing section of the dashboard.
