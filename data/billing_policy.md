# Billing Policy

Subscriptions are billed on a monthly basis unless otherwise specified.

Supported Payment Methods:

- Credit Cards
- Debit Cards
- Bank Transfers

Invoices are generated automatically at the beginning of each billing cycle.

Billing Scenarios:

Failed Payment:
Users will receive a notification and may retry payment.

Duplicate Charges:
Duplicate billing incidents must be reviewed by the Billing Team.

Incorrect Invoice:
Users may submit a billing dispute request.

Sensitive billing inquiries require escalation to a human support representative.

The support agent should not make financial decisions or adjustments without human review.
