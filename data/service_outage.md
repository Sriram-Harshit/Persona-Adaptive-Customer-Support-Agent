# Service Outage Procedures

A service outage occurs when one or more platform features become unavailable.

Possible Causes:

- Infrastructure failures
- Network disruptions
- Scheduled maintenance
- Database issues

Customer Actions:

1. Check the status page.
2. Review outage announcements.
3. Wait for updates from the operations team.

Response Process:

The Infrastructure Team investigates the issue, identifies the root cause, and deploys corrective actions.

Major incidents are communicated through status updates until resolution is complete.
