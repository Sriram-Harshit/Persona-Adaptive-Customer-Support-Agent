# Refund Policy

Refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Eligible Refund Scenarios:

- Duplicate charges
- Service activation failures
- Incorrect billing
- Technical issues preventing service usage

Refund Request Process:

1. Submit a refund request.
2. Provide invoice details.
3. Include supporting documentation.
4. Wait for review by the Billing Department.

Processing Time:

Most refund requests are reviewed within 5 to 10 business days.

All refund-related conversations must be escalated to a human support specialist.
