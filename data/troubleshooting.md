# General Troubleshooting Guide

Before contacting support, users should perform basic troubleshooting steps.

Recommended Actions:

1. Refresh the application.
2. Log out and log in again.
3. Clear browser cache and cookies.
4. Restart the browser.
5. Restart the device.
6. Verify internet connectivity.
7. Disable browser extensions.

If the issue persists after all troubleshooting steps, collect screenshots and error messages before contacting support.

Providing detailed information helps support teams diagnose issues more efficiently.
