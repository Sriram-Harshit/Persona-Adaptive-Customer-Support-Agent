# Password Reset Guide

Users who forget their password can reset it through the Forgot Password feature.

Steps:

1. Navigate to the Login page.
2. Click the Forgot Password link.
3. Enter the registered email address.
4. Submit the request.
5. Open the password reset email.
6. Click the reset link.
7. Create a new secure password.

Requirements:

- Password must contain at least 8 characters.
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters.
- Include at least one number.

Common Issues:

Issue: Reset email not received
Solution:

- Check spam folder.
- Verify email address.
- Wait 5 minutes before retrying.

Issue: Reset link expired
Solution:

- Generate a new reset request.
- Use the latest email received.

Issue: Account locked
Solution:

- Wait 15 minutes.
- Contact support for manual verification.

Security Notes:
Never share your password with anyone. Support agents will never ask for your password.
